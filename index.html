Researcher

Afonso Biscaia

[Instituto de Ciências Sociais - Universidade de Lisboa] PhD

I work in political science and political communication. I have published research on radical right-wing populism in Portugal in comparison with other countries. I am especially interested in the interaction between populist political communication and trust in political institutions and the news media. I mostly use qualitative methods.
